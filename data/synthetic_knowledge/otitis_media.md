# Otitis Media (Synthetic Medical Knowledge)

## What is it?
Otitis Media is a middle ear infection, often caused by bacteria or viruses. It affects the space behind the eardrum.

## Common symptoms
- Ear pain or pressure
- Fluid feeling or drainage from the ear
- Hearing difficulty
- Fever (sometimes)
- Irritability in children
- Trouble sleeping due to ear discomfort

## Why fluid accumulates
Infection or blockage causes inflammation and fluid buildup behind the eardrum. This fluid may drain outward if the eardrum is irritated or perforated.

## How it connects to common patient complaints
- "Fluid from ear" often matches **middle ear fluid** on a report
- Ear pain and fever suggest active infection
- Hearing difficulty may occur when fluid blocks sound transmission

## When to seek urgent care
- Severe or worsening ear pain
- High fever (above 102°F / 39°C)
- Swelling or redness behind the ear
- Stiff neck, confusion, or severe headache
- Symptoms not improving after prescribed treatment
