# Medical Glossary (Patient-Friendly)

| Medical Term | Simple Explanation |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Otitis Media | Ear infection in the middle part of the ear |
| Hypertension | High blood pressure |
| LDL | "Bad" cholesterol that can build up in arteries |
| HDL | "Good" cholesterol that helps clear excess cholesterol |
| HbA1c | Average blood sugar over about 3 months |
| Fasting Glucose | Blood sugar measured after not eating overnight |
| Dyslipidemia | Cholesterol or blood fats outside recommended range |
| Conductive hearing loss | Hearing difficulty due to blockage in outer or middle ear |
| Tympanic membrane | The eardrum |
| Effusion | Fluid buildup in a body space (here, the middle ear) |
| Microvascular changes | Small blood vessel changes, often chronic and mild on brain MRI |
| Ischemic changes | Changes related to reduced blood flow over time |
| Infarct | An area of tissue damage from loss of blood supply (stroke in the brain) |

## Important disclaimer
Definitions are simplified for education. Your doctor explains what terms mean for your specific report.
