# Type 2 Diabetes (Synthetic Medical Knowledge)

## What is it?
Type 2 diabetes is a condition where the body has difficulty using insulin properly, leading to higher blood sugar over time.

## Key lab terms patients see
| Term | Simple meaning |
|------|----------------|
| Fasting Glucose | Blood sugar after not eating overnight |
| HbA1c | Average blood sugar over about 3 months |
| Elevated | Higher than the usual target range |

## Common symptoms (may or may not be present)
- Increased thirst
- Frequent urination
- Fatigue
- Blurred vision (over time)
- Slow healing of cuts

## What elevated results may suggest
A report showing elevated glucose and HbA1c **may suggest** that blood sugar control needs attention. Only a doctor can interpret results in full context.

## General guidance (not treatment)
- Follow prescribed medications as directed by your doctor
- Discuss diet, exercise, and follow-up labs with your care team
- Do not stop or change medicines without medical advice
