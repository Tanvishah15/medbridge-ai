# Symptom-to-Finding Connections (Synthetic)

| Patient Symptom | Possible Report Finding | Connection |
|----------------|------------------------|------------|
| Ear discharge | Middle ear fluid | Infection can cause fluid buildup; fluid may leak outward |
| Ear pain | Otitis Media | Inflammation in the middle ear causes pain |
| Hearing difficulty | Conductive hearing reduction | Fluid or blockage can reduce sound transmission |
| Frequent thirst | Elevated glucose | High blood sugar can increase thirst |
| Frequent urination | Elevated glucose / HbA1c | Body tries to remove excess sugar through urine |
| Blurred vision | Elevated HbA1c | Poor long-term sugar control can affect eyes over time |
| Fatigue | Elevated glucose | Uncontrolled blood sugar may cause tiredness |
| Headache | Hypertension | High blood pressure can sometimes cause headaches |
| Dizziness | Hypertension / blood sugar changes | Multiple causes — doctor evaluates full context |

## Clarifying questions MedBridge may ask
- How many days have symptoms been present?
- Is there fever or severe pain?
- Are symptoms getting worse?
- Is the patient taking prescribed medicines as directed?

## Important disclaimer
Connections are educational patterns, not a diagnosis. Always follow your doctor's advice.
