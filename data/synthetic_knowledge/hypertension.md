# Hypertension (Synthetic Medical Knowledge)

## What is it?
Hypertension means **high blood pressure** — the force of blood against artery walls is higher than recommended over time.

## Why it matters
Long-term high blood pressure can strain the heart, kidneys, brain, and blood vessels if not managed with a doctor.

## Common terms on reports
| Term | Simple meaning |
|------|----------------|
| Systolic | Pressure when heart beats |
| Diastolic | Pressure when heart rests |
| Stage 1 / Stage 2 | Categories doctors use to describe severity |

## Possible symptoms (often none early on)
- Headaches
- Dizziness
- Nosebleeds (sometimes)
- Many people have no symptoms until checked

## When to seek urgent care
- Chest pain
- Severe headache with vision changes
- Shortness of breath
- Blood pressure extremely high with symptoms (follow local emergency guidance)

## Important disclaimer
This is educational information only. Always follow your doctor's advice.
