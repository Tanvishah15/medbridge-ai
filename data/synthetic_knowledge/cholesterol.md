# Cholesterol & LDL (Synthetic Medical Knowledge)

## What is cholesterol?
Cholesterol is a fatty substance in the blood. The body needs some, but too much of certain types can affect heart and blood vessel health over time.

## Key terms on lab reports
| Term | Simple meaning |
|------|----------------|
| LDL | Often called "bad" cholesterol — can build up in arteries |
| HDL | Often called "good" cholesterol — helps remove excess cholesterol |
| Triglycerides | Another type of blood fat |
| Dyslipidemia | Cholesterol/fats outside recommended ranges |

## What elevated LDL may suggest
An elevated LDL on a report **may suggest** increased cardiovascular risk when considered with other factors. A doctor interprets the full picture (age, blood pressure, diabetes, family history).

## General guidance (not treatment)
- Discuss diet, activity, and medication options with your doctor
- Follow up on repeat labs as recommended
- Do not start or stop cholesterol medicines without medical advice

## Important disclaimer
This is educational information only. Always follow your doctor's advice.
